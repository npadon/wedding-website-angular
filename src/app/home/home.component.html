OUR STORY

Emily was living in New York City and working at a consulting firm; and Nick lived in Houston and worked for the same consulting firm. On a work trip to Miami, they met for the first time in a crowded bar and talked about their favorite books (Nick was being exceptionally awkward). A few months later, they crossed paths on a project in New Orleans and became friends. Call it luck or fate, Emily had been planning to one day move to Houston. Once she did, it didn’t take Nick long to desperately try to be with her...which is now going to be forever.
